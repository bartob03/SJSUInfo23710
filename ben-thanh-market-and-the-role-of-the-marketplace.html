Ben Thanh Market and the role of the "Marketplace"

July 8, 2023

Ben Thanh market is often included in Ho Chih Minh / Saigon travel guides. In the podcast The Vietnamese Narrative, Nhan Phan, a Viet native, talks about the history of Ben Thanh and the general role of the marketplace in Vietnamese culture.

Origin

Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam

 

The Vietnamese Narrative

Most tourists visit a location without bothering to do any research whatsoever about their destination. They tick off their checklist, buy souvenirs, shop, and consider themselves "travelers" afterwards. So much is lost to the typical tourist. This behavior is most common in Asia, where Westerners travel for the sake of traveling. Vietnam, a country which has changed drastically in the last decade, is one of those places that deserve due diligence before visiting.

Ben Thanh market offers nothing of interest at first glance - it offers very little to someone who has visited any dry or wet market. Nhan Phan argues that the market has its fair share of historical value and has weathered many, many years of change, and yet somehow remains the same.
