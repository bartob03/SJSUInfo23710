Ben Thanh Market and the role of the "Marketplace"

By Brian Bartolome
July 8, 2023

Ben Thanh market is often listed in Ho Chih Minh travel guides as one of the places that deserve a quick look. In the podcast The Vietnamese Narrative, Nhan Phan, a Viet native, talks about the history of the market and the general role of the marketplace in Vietnamese culture.

Origin

Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam

 

The Vietnamese Narrative

Most tourists visit a location without bothering to do any research whatsoever about their destination. They tick off their list, buy souvenirs, shop, and consider themselves "travelers" afterwards. So much is lost to the typical tourist because of laziness and apathy. This behavior is most common in Asia, where Westerners travel for the sake of traveling only. Vietnam, a country which has changed drastically in the last decade, is one of those places that deserve due diligence before visiting. Ben Thanh market offers nothing of interest at first glance - it offers very little to someone has visited any Asian dry or wet market.

Nhan Phan in his podcast shows that the market has weathered many, many years of change, and yet somehow remains the same.
