The Neighborhood Pump Attendant

July 8, 2023

While it is true Singapore is considered one of the wealthiest city-states in Asia, global media coverage rarely focuses on parts of the society that do not indulge in the wealthy excesses of the Singapore elite. This short clip from CNA Insider focuses on one of these residents: the hard-working gas pump attendant.

Origin

Singapore

 

The other sight of Singapore

Singapore is small, but extremely diverse. There are wealthy expatriates from all over the world, immigrants from different parts of Asia, and Malays with mixed heritage. Singapore has retained its dubious reputation as a "nanny state", but the name of the country is also associated with a technocratic and grossly wealthy population. This idea is somewhat misleading as the now-crowded city state is characterized by a large wealth gap between foreigners/elite and the struggling working class.
