The Neighborhood Pump Attendant

By Brian Bartolome
July 8, 2023

While it is true Singapore is considered one of the wealthiest city-states in Asia, global media coverage rarely focuses on parts of the society that do not indulge in the wealthy excesses of the Singapore elite. This short clip from CNA Insider focuses on one of these residents: the hard-working gas pump attendant.

Origin

Singapore

 

The other sight of Singapore

Singapore is small, but extremely diverse. There are wealthy expatriates from all over the world, immigrants from different parts of Asia, and Malays with mixed heritage. As with other Southeast Asian countries, Singapore has its own dubious reputation as a "nanny state", though the name of the country is always accompanied with the idea of a technocratic and grossly wealthy population. Unfortunately, the truth is very different as the now-crowded city state is characterized by a large wealth gap between foreigners and the elite, and the struggling working class.
