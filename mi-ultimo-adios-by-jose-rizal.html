Mi Ultimo Adios by Jose Rizal

By Brian Bartolome
July 8, 2023

Overview

Jose Rizal is the Philippine’s national hero and prominent writer advocating Spanish rule reform. "Mi Ultimo Adios" is Jose Rizal’s final Spanish poem before his execution after his arrest for sedition.  

Origin

Manila, Philippines

 

"My Final Farewell" in English

You can read Rizal's last work from the Jose Rizal website.
