Pavin's book about monarchy banned in Thailand

By Brian Bartolome
July 8, 2023

This Bangkok Post news article shows the hazards of criticizing the Thai monarchy and the nature of censorship in countries such as Thailand. 

Origin

Book banning in Thailand

Thailand is notorious for strict rules regarding criticizing the government and/or the monarchy. This short article demonstrates the consequences of writing a book that includes any negative comments about both.

Bangkok Post. (2023, July 8). Pavin's book about monarchy banned in Thailand. Retrieved from Bangkok Post: https://www.bangkokpost.com/thailand/general/2608113/pavins-book-about-monarchy-banned-in-thailand

Thailand has built a mixed reputation for itself because of its tourist industry, but there is so much more to the country than its beaches, red-light district, Thai women, massages and food. Foreigners frequently abuse Thai culture and the word itself elicits a poor knee-jerk reaction. As with many Southeast Asian countries, however, Thailand is unique and vibrant with much of its literature and art reflecting a completely different world.
