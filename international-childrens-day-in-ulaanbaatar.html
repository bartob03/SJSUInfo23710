International Children's Day in Ulaanbaatar

July 8, 2023

International Children's Day in Ulaanbataar is celebrated every year. This news photo gallery from the Mongolian National News Agency shows how residents of Ulaanbataar believe children deserve an official holiday each year.  

Origin

 

Celebrating children

It doesn't matter if a country is impoverished, developing or developed, but Asians always dedicate one day towards celebrating children. While religion, economics, and culture play a part in valuing family, there is little doubt that in countries such as Mongolia, the children really are the future.
