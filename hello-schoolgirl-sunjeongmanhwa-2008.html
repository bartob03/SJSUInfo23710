Hello Schoolgirl /  순정만화 (2008)

July 8, 2023

Hello Schoolgirl / 순정만화 (2008) is a charming, light-hearted comedy that follows two May-December relationships. In one, a shy public servant in his 30s develops a singular friendship with his neighbor, an 18-year old high school student. In the other, a lovely bereaved photographer catches the eye of an immature, brash young man. 

Origin

Seoul, South Korea

 

Soo-Young and Kim Yeon-woo Meet

South Korea has somehow overtaken Japan as the most popular source of Asian music and entertainment. From K-pop to K-drama, the Korean wave (Hallyu) has slowly seeped into European and North American culture. Movies and music, however, can often be misleading and some of the cultural nuances are lost for the sake of entertainment. Work ethics, treatment of homosexuals, and May-December relationships are just some of the dynamics that are quite different from the West. Movies such as "Hello Schoolgirl", while providing light entertainment, provide a small peek into the inner workings of South Korean society.
