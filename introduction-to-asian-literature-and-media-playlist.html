Introduction to Asian Literature and Media playlist 

July 8, 2023

This playlist provides an overview of how teachers and students can be introduced to Asian literature and media. The objective of the playlist is to elicit learning and curiosity towards Asian history and literature via mixed media. In doing so, learners can be better prepared in communicating with Asians and dealing with misconceptions and ignorance about their culture. Moreover, by developing a rich appreciation of the culture, learners are equipped to handle negative outcomes caused by prejudices and hate.

This playlist is designed to meet the competencies and domains as they are described in the AASL Standards Framework for Learners.
