Hear Me / 聽說 (2009)

By Brian Bartolome
July 8, 2023

Overview

Hear Me / 聽說 (2009) revolves around a food delivery boy, Tien Kuo, who develops a crush on Yang Yang, a hard-working girl devoted to her deaf and mute sister. Tien Kuo quickly realizes that although his Taiwan Sign Language (TSL) skills are more than adequate to communicate with Yang Yang, there is more to communication than language. 

Origin

Taipei, Taiwan
