Overview

Shigeru Mizuki’s Showa series is a semi-autobiographical series of manga that covers not only the major events in Japanese history but also smaller less well-known incidents such as the Tokyo subway sarin attack and an 80s crime ring that threatened to poison candy.  

Origin

Tokyo, Japan
