The Riot by Khushwant Singh

By Brian Bartolome
July 8, 2023

Khushwant Singh was a well-known Indian writer and political commentator.  His stories often took on themes of conflict within Indian society. “The Riot” is one of the stories included in “The Collected Short Stories of Khushwant Singh”.  

Origin

Hadali, Pakistan

 

"The Riot" short story

"The Riot" is an example of a very subtle commentary on politics and religion in South Asia. The highly stratified and violent environment of the time when the story was written made it difficult to address societal problems directly. Singh, like many proactive writers, tried to get his political messages across through his stories.  

"The Riot" can be read online from the SJSU library.
